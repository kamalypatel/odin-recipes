Pizza Recipie

Description

Pizza is an amazing feat of man kind. Humans everywhere seem to love it.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Cubed Pineapple Pieces

Steps

  1. Pre-Heat oven to 450 degrees farenheit
  2. Roll out the dough in a circle
  3. Put sauce on dough
  4. Put cheese on top of dough with sauce
  5. Put pineapple on top of the cheese
  6. Put pizza in oven for 12 mins once oven is done pre-heating
  7. Take pizza out after the 12 mins. Let cool
  8. Enjoy with friends!
